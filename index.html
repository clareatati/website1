Development of an AI-Driven Speed Governing and Photogrammetry System

To Reduce Road Accidents on Uhuru Highway, Nairobi, Kenya

Main Objective

To develop and implement an AI-driven speed governor and photogrammetry system to reduce road accidents on Uhuru Highway by automatically controlling vehicle speed and monitoring lane adherence.

Specific Objectives

  • Integrate existing GIS data with real-time photogrammetric data.
  • Improve vehicle speed estimation accuracy using photogrammetry and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Detect lane deviations and issue timely alerts to the system and the speed governor.
